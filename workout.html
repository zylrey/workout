30-Day Chest Workout Plan

Preparation and Guidelines

Warm-Up (5 minutes): Perform arm circles, push-ups (modified if needed), and light cardio like jumping jacks.

Workout Tips:

  • Perform 3 sets of each exercise, resting for 30–60 seconds between sets.
  • Complete the workout 3–4 days per week, allowing at least 1 rest day between sessions.
  • Stretch your chest and shoulders after each session to improve flexibility and recovery.
Day Exercise Reps Sets Description
1 Dumbbell Chest Press 10–12 3 Lie on your back, press dumbbells upward, then lower slowly.
1 Dumbbell Chest Fly 10–12 3 Open arms wide, keeping elbows slightly bent, then bring them back together.
1 Dumbbell Pullover 10–12 3 Hold a dumbbell with both hands, lower it backward over your head, and return.
2 Rest/Stretch - - Light stretches focusing on chest and shoulders.
3 Dumbbell Incline Press 10–12 3 Lie at an incline, press dumbbells upward, and lower slowly.
3 Dumbbell Squeeze Press 10–12 3 Hold dumbbells together, press upward, and lower while squeezing tightly.
3 Dumbbell Chest Fly 10–12 3 Same as Day 1.
4 Rest/Stretch - - Light stretches or yoga for recovery.
5 Repeat Day 1 Exercises 10–12 3 -
6 Rest/Stretch - - -
7 Light Chest Stretch/Yoga - - Focus on flexibility and chest mobility.
8 Progress to Slow Tempo Reps 10–12 3 Perform exercises from Day 1 but slow the lowering phase to 3–4 seconds.
9 Rest/Stretch - - -
10 Combine Press & Fly Supersets 10–12 3 Alternate Chest Press and Chest Fly without resting between.
11 Rest/Stretch - - -
12 Repeat Day 3 Exercises 10–12 3 -
13 Rest/Stretch - - -
14 Light Active Recovery - - Gentle yoga or dynamic stretches.
